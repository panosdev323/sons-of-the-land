Terms of Service

Welcome to "Sons of the Land". We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

1. Information We Collect

Sons of the Land stores game progress locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This includes:

  • Your global score
  • Civilization progress and level completion
  • High scores per civilization

2. Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device only. We:

  • Do NOT send your data to external servers
  • Do NOT share your data with third parties
  • Do NOT collect personal information
  • Do NOT use analytics or tracking

3. Data Safety

  • Data is stored only in your browser's local storage
  • No personal information is collected
  • No third-party tracking or analytics
  • You can clear your data anytime by clearing your browser storage or app cache

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Your continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of any updates.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
