Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sons of the Land. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

Sons of the Land stores game progress locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage).

  • Your global score
  • Civilization progress and level completion
  • High scores per civilization

2. Advertising

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect limited technical information automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Crash and performance data
  • General usage information

This information is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

We do not:

  • Send your personal data to our own servers
  • Sell your personal information
  • Require account registration

4. Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

5. Data Safety

  • Most game data is stored locally on your device
  • No sensitive personal information is collected by us directly
  • You can clear local game data anytime through your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

This game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the game means you accept any updates.
