Privacy Policy

Sons of the Land  ·  Effective:

Welcome to Sons of the Land. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1 Information We Collect

Sons of the Land stores game progress locally on your device using browser or device storage. We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. The following game data is stored locally:

  • Your global score
  • Civilization progress and level completion
  • High scores per civilization

2 Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. AdMob may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID)
  • Crash and performance data
  • General usage and interaction information

This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You may opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings.

3 Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Transmit your personal data to our own servers
  • Sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Require you to create an account or register

You can delete all locally stored data at any time through your device or browser settings.

4 Third-Party Services

The game may integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

5 Data Safety

  • Game data is stored locally on your device and not shared with us
  • No sensitive personal information is collected by us directly
  • No data encryption is applied to locally stored game data
  • You can clear all local game data through your device settings at any time

6 Children's Privacy

Sons of the Land does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the game following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
